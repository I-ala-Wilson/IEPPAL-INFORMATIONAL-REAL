Learning Support Done Better With IEPPAL

At IEPPAL our main focus is on nurturing students into confident self-advocates with the help of teachers and parents.

IEPPAL intends to revolutionize learning support by centralizing IEPs into one intuitive platform. More importantly, IEPPAL gives space for educators and students to form authentic relationships and for students to recognize their achievements over time.

What's an IEP?

An Individualized Education Plan (IEP) is a vital document in special education that outlines students' learning needs, services, and progress measures, ensuring personalized support to bridge the gap between their unique requirements and academic goals.

What's the issue here?

Your best pal in learning support

IEPPAL comes batteries included. It takes the best parts of state-of-the-art tools and adds its own innovations.

Progress Tracking and Reporting

IEPPAL will provide automated, customizable progress reports that are generated based on student data.

Built For Your Convenience

IEPPAL's digital infrastructure will be tailored for an intuitive and pragmatic feel, based on the opinions of 20+ industry members.

Leveraging AI in Goal-Setting

IEPPAL will understand a student's needs and help form personalized goals to best help them grow.

Broad Integration

IEPPAL will integrate with popular educational softwares such as Veracross and Google Classroom for ease of use and application in a school setting.

Seamless Collaboration

IEPPAL will be built to prioritise the ease of collaboration between all relevant stakeholders, including teachers, parents, and learning support case managers.

Assisted Accountability

IEPPAL will help you keep track of what you need to do and when, ensuring your students can be best supported.

Synthesizing Key Information

IEPPAL will synthesize key information on students' self-efficacy, parent input, teacher feedback, diagnosis, along with several other measures that paint a holistic picture.

Building Confident Self-Advocates

IEPPAL will provide a creative medium where students can create video recordings and other types of journaling that track growth over time.
